Fast And Accurate Human Detection

Find Intruder Immediately

In our project, we use yolo as the main model to detect human. When there is a human walking through the car, the thermal detector will make Rpi3 take a shot and make detection. We've test the feature several times, the average detection time is 1.5s/image. Simple, fast, and accurately.

Safe and Sound

A Reliable Defender

I.S.G. automatically patrols region of interest specified by the user and detect any possible intruders. You can easily set up any desirable route in advance by maneuvering I.S.G. directly. When I.S.G. stops and raises an alarm, you may decide on whether it should further call the police or just ignore and keep going.
